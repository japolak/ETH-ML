# Frequency-domain Features (Fourier Transformation and Power Spectral Density)
12. Dominant frequency estimate
13. Average frequency (From PSD)
14. Factoral Exponent (Slope of linear fit of PSD in loglog graph)
15. Spectral Edge Density / Integral (Intensity below which 95% of total power of signal is located)
16. Spectral Moment zero Crossings
17. Spectral Moment extrema per sec
18. Spectral Entropy
19. Renyi Entropy
20. Bandpower (Total power in .25 - 40 Hz)
21. Delta waves (.25 - 4 Hz)
22. Theta waves (4 - 8 Hz)
23. Alpha waves (8 - 12 Hz)
24. Sigma waves (12 - 16 Hz)
25. Beta waves  (16 - 30 Hz)
26. Gamma waves (30+ Hz)

(Note, you can additionally compute Power Ratios (eg Delta/Theta and similar))
